Our Story

Built on trust, scaled by quality.

Adrijen Healthcare was founded with a clear belief — that genuine quality, fair pricing and on-time supply are the only things distributors and chemists really need from a pharma supplier. We started small, with a focused product list and a handful of partners, and have grown into a trusted name across multiple states.

Today our portfolio covers 20 product categories — from everyday tablets and syrups to specialised injections, ointments, dental, ophthalmic and ayurvedic ranges. Every batch is manufactured in WHO-GMP and ISO 9001:2015 certified facilities under strict in-process and finished-product quality control.

What we stand for

Vision, Mission & Quality

Our Vision

To contribute to society's well-being by making safe, effective and affordable pharmaceutical products available wherever they are needed — backed by uncompromising quality control and full regulatory compliance.

We see every distributor relationship as a long-term partnership built on trust, transparency and shared success.

Our Mission

To improve healthcare access across India by delivering high-quality medicines and healthcare products through a reliable distributor network — at prices that reach the patient who needs them most.

We are committed to ethical business, continuous innovation and addressing unmet medical needs in everyday Indian healthcare.

Our Quality

All Adrijen products are manufactured under strict WHO-GMP guidelines with batch-level QC documentation. We test for safety, efficacy and purity so that every chemist and patient gets a medicine they can trust.

Our culture of compliance is the simplest reason distributors keep reordering from us.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Adrijen.

Every milestone is a promise kept — to our distributors, prescribers and the patients we ultimately serve.

2019 — The Beginning

Adrijen Healthcare launched with a small but focused antibiotic and analgesic portfolio.

2020 — WHO-GMP & ISO Certification

Our manufacturing partners achieved WHO-GMP and ISO 9001:2015 certification across product lines.

2022 — 100+ SKUs & National Distribution

Crossed 100 product SKUs and onboarded distributors across 50+ cities.

2024 — 20 Product Categories

Expanded into ointments, dental, ophthalmic, protein and ayurvedic formats, completing our 20-category portfolio.

2026 — 157 Products & 100+ Cities

Adrijen products are now stocked in over 1,500 distributor outlets across 100+ Indian cities.
